Pratyush Sharma is a psychological thriller author and Computer Science student known for his debut novel, The Price of Silence: A Delhi Noir His work focuses on the intersection of institutional corruption and moral ambiguity, examining the complex power dynamics of urban environments. Writing through the lens of a "psychological anatomist," Pratyush explores the visceral pressure that systemic decay exerts on the human conscience.

Pratyush Sharma is an author and Computer Science student who integrates technical logic with psychological storytelling. His work utilizes concepts such as entropy and algorithmic pacing to craft precise, high-tension thrillers. Based in Delhi, Pratyush’s fiction explores the intersection of systematic structures and human narrative, creating a distinct, analytical approach to the genre.

His debut novel, The Price of Silence: A Delhi Noir, published by Notion Press in early 2026, follows Detective Harivansh Oberoi through a capital city where bribes move faster than ambulances, and where silence is never free.

He is a member within the Alfaaz Creative Writing Society at ADGIPS College, and is currently drafting his second manuscript, Kamakti.

Pratyush Sharma was born and raised in Delhi. A city that, for him, was never just a setting. It was a character. A system. A verdict waiting to be written.

He is currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science, studying Java, DBMS, Python, SQL, HTML, and CSS. Not as an escape from storytelling, but as an extension of it. His technical interests in Edge AI, TinyML, entropy modelling, and algorithmic narrative structures have profoundly shaped how he constructs stories.

His debut novel, The Price of Silence: A Delhi Noir, was published by Notion Press in late 2025 and distributed nationally through Atlantic Publishers. The book introduces Detective Harivansh Oberoi and asks: at what point does silence become complicity?

Pratyush practises pencil sketching and anatomical illustration. He is deeply influenced by Dostoevsky's psychological excavations, Kafka's existential machinery, and Kant's categorical moral frameworks. His cinematic sensibility draws from Atonement and Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind.

He is an active member of the Alfaaz Creative Writing Society at ADGIPS College. His second manuscript, Kamakti, is currently in development.

002 — Published Works

The Library.

Debut Novel · 2025

The Price
of Silence

Delhi Noir · Psychological Thriller · Crime Fiction

Delhi never forgets, and Detective Harivansh Oberoi knows that every case in this city comes with a debt. He patrols a capital where bribes move faster than ambulances and hospitals decide who survives by who can pay. His quiet routine fractures when a brutal case lands on his desk, revealing a chain of deaths as chilling as they are intentional.

At its heart stands Delhi Hospital — the city's monument to corruption — where morality, grief, and justice collide in a brutal war of conscience. The Price of Silence merges detective work, investigative journalism, and the shadow of a relentless figure whose methods force everyone to confront the line where justice ends and vengeance begins.

004 — Influences

The Canon.

Literary · Philosophy
Fyodor Dostoevsky
Crime and Punishment, The Brothers Karamazov
The godfather of psychological depth. Dostoevsky understood that guilt is not a moral verdict — it is a living thing, an organism that reshapes the interior of whoever carries it.
Literary · Existentialism
Franz Kafka
The Trial, The Metamorphosis
Kafka mapped institutional dread before institutions admitted to being dreadful. His systems — opaque, unyielding, absurd — are not metaphors. They are blueprints.
Philosophy · Ethics
Immanuel Kant
Critique of Pure Reason, Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals
The categorical imperative as a weapon — a standard that every morally grey character in his fiction quietly fails against.
Cinema · Drama
Atonement
Dir. Joe Wright, 2007
A study in the violence of perspective. How a single misread moment can fracture every life it touches. The long Dunkirk tracking shot is a masterclass in sustained dread.
Cinema · Romance / Existential
Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind
Dir. Michel Gondry, 2004
Memory as architecture. The ethics of erasure, the fragility of constructed identity, and what survives the deletion of experience.
Technical · Narrative
Algorithmic Story Theory
Entropy Models · Edge AI · TinyML
Computer science as craft tool. Entropy modelling to pace tension arcs, algorithmic character state machines — plots that are structurally airtight and feel inevitable.
005 — Craft

Code as Craft.

Pratyush approaches narrative architecture the way a software engineer approaches a distributed system: with obsessive attention to dependencies, failure states, and the behaviour of components under pressure.

His B.Tech studies in Computer Science — spanning Java, Python, DBMS, SQL, HTML/CSS — have rewired how he thinks about story structure. A plot is a schema. A character is a state machine.

His research into Edge AI and TinyML deepens this further — how constrained intelligence mirrors the constrained moral agency of characters trapped in corrupt systems.
